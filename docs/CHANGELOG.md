## v4.3.1 — Material & Tuning Expansion

### Expanded component options
- Block, crank, connecting rods, pistons: added Heavy/Light variants for finer weight and durability
- Head material: new Eco/Std/Perf tiers for both cast iron and aluminum, plus Aluminum Billet Race
- Injection: added Mechanical Injection and Single Point EFI
- Intake and exhaust manifolds: expanded with Low/Mid/High tiers
- Fuel: added Regular 91, Super 98, Leaded Gasoline, Compressed Gas and Nitromethane (extremely high power ceiling, high risk)

### New mechanical systems
- Continuously adjustable balancer weight (0-50 kg)
- VVL redesigned to 3 states (None/VVL/CVVL) with its own profile and switching speeds, smooth curve blending
- New valve spring stiffness model (valve float at high speeds)
- New sliders for carburetor/flap, intake ports and manifold sizes
- Fuel map (lean/rich mixture) as a separate parameter
- Exhaust bypass flaps (bypass above 3500 RPM)

### Other
- Fixed gear ratio for AWD preset adjusted
- More compact app window
- Backwards compatibility with older saved .json files (old VVL format will be automatically converted)
